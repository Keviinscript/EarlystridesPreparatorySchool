Earlystrides Preparatory School

At Earlystrides, we bring together all the indispensable components of a supportive, motivating and comprehensive learning experience for all students.

Enroll Now Enquires

Earlystirdes is a school located at Duste Junction in Kubwa, Abuja. Founded in 2010, we are a school committed to providing high quality teaching for all our children. We do this by doing weekly tests on core subjects, especially mathematics and science.

The goal of Earlystrides is to develop confident, disciplined and enquiring learners who are able to make informed choices. As well as, raising godly, well-spoken and respectful students who are skilled in mathematics and technology.

Our qualified and well-experienced staff help to promote an encouraging and high-spirited environment, we instill our children with high standards of hardwork and diligence which raises children that are independent and have high self-esteem.

We have classes from the early years till SS3 and are Junior WAEC/BECE and SSCE (WAEC/NECO) accredited. The school is also equipped with a wide range of laboratories and facilities such as a physics lab, chemistry lab, biology lab, a library and well-conditioned classrooms.

At Earlystrides, our children come first. We offer a secure and enjoyable work environment where kids are free to grow without facing prejudice due to their sex, gender, race, religion, language, ability, or social class.
